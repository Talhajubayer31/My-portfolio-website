#### CORE_LOGIC // Q&A [STRATEGY_INSIGHTS]

Q:

How do you determine which ad format to deploy?

A:

It's never about guessing; it's about intent. If we need immediate sales from people already looking for a solution, I deploy Search Ads. If we are building a brand and triggering desire, I use Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Video Ads with a heavy focus on storytelling and psychological triggers.

Q:

What makes a Meta Ad actually convert?

A:

A high-converting ad is a balance of three things: a 'Scroll-Stopping' creative, a 'Problem-Solving' copy, and Surgical Targeting. I focus on the 'Hook-Body-CTA' framework to ensure we aren't just getting likes, but capturing leads.

Q:

Why is a data-driven approach better than creative intuition?

A:

Intuition tells you what might work; data tells you what is working. By tracking metrics like ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) and CPA (Cost Per Acquisition), I can kill underperforming ads early and scale the winners, ensuring no part of your budget is wasted.

Q:

How do you bridge the gap between SEO and Paid Ads?

A:

They are two sides of the same coin. I use Paid Ads for instant velocity and SEO for long-term compounding growth. By analyzing high-performing keywords from ads, I optimize the website's content to dominate organic search results over time.

Q:

Can you handle the technical side of the funnel?

A:

Absolutely. An ad is only as good as the landing page it leads to. I integrate CMS-based web structures with tracking pixels and optimized UI/UX to ensure the transition from 'Click' to 'Customer' is seamless and frictionless.
